01

Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in, we collect your email address and password to authenticate your identity. Your name and employee ID are retrieved from your organization's employee management system linked to your account.

1.2 Location Data (Precise)

When you clock in or clock out, the App collects your precise GPS location (latitude and longitude). Location is collected only at the moment of clock-in or clock-out — the App does not continuously track your location in the background.

1.3 Attendance Records

We record the timestamp and location coordinates of each clock-in and clock-out event, along with your employee ID.

1.4 Local Device Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device in encrypted app-private storage (not accessible to other applications):

  • Authentication tokens (access token, refresh token)
  • Your email address and password (encrypted) — used to automatically re-authenticate when your session expires, so you do not need to sign in repeatedly
  • Your user ID and linked employee ID
  • Current clock-in status

All locally stored data is permanently deleted when you sign out of the App.

02

Android Permissions

The App requests the following Android permissions:

ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION
Required to record precise GPS coordinates at clock-in and clock-out for attendance verification.
ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION
Fallback when fine location is unavailable; same purpose as above.
POST_NOTIFICATIONS
Required on Android 13+ to display an ongoing notification while you are clocked in.
FOREGROUND_SERVICE
Keeps the app active while clocked in to maintain the attendance session.
INTERNET
Required to communicate with our backend server for authentication and attendance recording.

You may revoke any of these permissions at any time through your device's Settings. Revoking location permission will prevent the App from recording new attendance entries, but previously recorded attendance remains unaffected.

03

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating your identity and managing your session
  • Recording attendance with location verification
  • Displaying your attendance status within the App
  • Automatically restoring your session after it expires (to improve user experience)
  • Providing your employer with attendance data for payroll and workforce management purposes

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose not listed above.

04

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. We do not share data with advertisers or analytics providers.

Your data is transmitted exclusively to the Kinnavi backend server operated by Scient Labs and made available to your employer (the organization that provided your account) for legitimate workforce management purposes.

The App uses Google Play Services on your device to obtain location readings. No personal data or location data is shared with Google through this usage — Google Play Services operates locally on your device.

05

Data Security

  • In transit: All data transmitted between the App and our server is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
  • On device: Authentication tokens and credentials are stored using Android's EncryptedSharedPreferences (AES-256 encryption), within the app's sandboxed private storage.
  • On server: Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms before storage. Data is protected by access controls and security monitoring.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

06

Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained on your device until you sign out, at which point all stored tokens, credentials, and session data are permanently deleted.
  • Attendance records: Retained on our server in accordance with your employer's data retention policy and applicable labor laws (typically 3–7 years under Japanese labor law).
  • Account data: Retained while your account remains active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request, subject to legal retention obligations.
07

Account & Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and personal data, please email info@scient-labs.com with the subject line "Kinnavi Data Deletion Request" from the email address registered with your Kinnavi account.

Your request will be processed within 30 days. The following data will be deleted:

  • Your user account and authentication credentials
  • Personal identification information (name, email, employee ID linkage)
  • Location coordinates associated with your attendance records

The following data may be retained in anonymized form for legal compliance:

  • Aggregated attendance records required by Japanese labor law (retained for statutory periods, then deleted)
  • System audit logs required for security and fraud prevention

You may also sign out from within the App at any time to immediately delete all locally stored data from your device.

08

Your Rights

Under Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI) and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by us
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your personal data (see Section 07)
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (may result in account termination)
  • Lodge a complaint with Japan's Personal Information Protection Commission (PPC)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 12.

09

Children's Privacy

The App is designed exclusively for workplace use by employed adults and is not intended for or directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly.

10

International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in Japan. If you access the App from outside Japan, your data will be transferred to and processed in Japan. By using the App, you consent to this transfer. We ensure adequate protection for cross-border data transfers in compliance with APPI requirements.
